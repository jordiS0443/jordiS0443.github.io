Apple Website Project

In this Copycat Project,I chose to recreate the Apple support website.

Throughout this project I have learned and practiced alot of CSS and HTML from the knowledege that was gained through Freecodecamp and zoom calls . What I would say were the struggles throughout working on my project were the positioning the various amounts of logos,text and searchbar in a proper way. But I overcame these challenges and roadblocks by doing research and going back to my notes and looking at online resource like W3 schools. Still, I know that there is more room for improvement and how that this will be a motivation for me to learn and practice often as we progess into these newer topics. Besides what has been mentioned, I have learned alot when working on this project. And those topics include, nav links, inserting a searchbar, putting a background image, and learning some filter elements. Overall, it was a pretty neat project to work on.
