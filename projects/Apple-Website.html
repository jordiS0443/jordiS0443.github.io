Apple Website Project

In this Copycat Project,I chose to recreate the Apple support website.

Throughout this project, I have learned and practiced a lot of CSS and HTML from the knowledge that was gained through Freecodecamp and zoom calls. What I would say were the struggles throughout working on my project were positioning the various amounts of logos, text, and search bar correctly. But I overcame these challenges and roadblocks by doing research and going back to my notes and looking at an online resource like W3 schools. Still, I know that there is more room for improvement and how that this will be a motivation for me to learn and practice often as we progress into these newer topics. Besides what has been mentioned, I have learned a lot when working on this project. And those topics include nav links, inserting a search bar, putting a background image, and learning some filter elements. Overall, it was a pretty neat project to work on.
