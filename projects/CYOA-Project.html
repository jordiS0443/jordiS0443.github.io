CYOA Project

The COYA (Create your own adventure) was a project that assigned us to collaborate together with a group and use our knowledge of github and the command line to create an immersive story

Throughout this project many challenges were faced that confused and puzzled the group as a whole. These challenges include, corrrectly linking the html files to another html file, and merge conflicts. Though these challenge was timely and sometimes difficult, it didnt stop our group to collaborate as a team and fully solve the issue together.

If there was more time available for the project, I would continue to add more scenarios into the story and create this complex and riveting story that has the reader to want to dive in deeper.
